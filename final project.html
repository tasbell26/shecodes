Have You Seen the Mothman?


The Man the Myth and the Legend of Point Pleasant, West Virginia

The Mothman of Point Pleasant, West Virginia is a creature of infamous reputation. That for decades has been the center of fear, speculation and surprisingly, tourism to hundreds of locals since the mid 60's. First seen following two young couples in their car, to being blamed for the Silver Bridge collapse claiming 46 lives. Though the Mothman is steeped in mystery it has become an unusual beacon of revenue for the town where it hosts a Mothman festival every year in September. In recent years it seems Mothman has expanded his territory with sightings being reported in Texas and as far as Moscow. With so little known of this creature and its ever expanding reach there's no telling where it will been seen next. So... Have you seen the Mothman?
